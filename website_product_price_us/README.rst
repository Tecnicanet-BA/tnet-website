in the pricelist rules.

**Table of contents**

.. contents::
   :local:
   
Configuration
=============

#. In the product model, we must have created a field called *x_studio_precio_en_us*
#. Go to *Website > Products > Pricelists > Pricelist > Price Rules > Set Based on Precio en US*
